Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

Important: This extension does not collect, sell, or share any user data. All data is stored locally in your browser.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The Pinterest Fullscreen Chrome Extension operates entirely locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.sync) to save your preferences, including:

  • Toggle states for hiding/showing UI elements
  • Hover threshold values (top and left side)
  • Custom background color preferences

All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. Your preferences remain on your device and are never sent to us or any other party.

4. Data Usage

The extension only accesses Pinterest.com to provide its functionality. It does not:

  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Collect analytics
  • Monitor your activity
  • Access your Pinterest account information
  • Read or modify your Pinterest content

5. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To inject content scripts on Pinterest pages for hiding/showing navigation elements
  • storage: To save your preferences locally in your browser
  • pinterest.com host permission: To run only on Pinterest websites

These permissions are used solely for the extension's functionality and nothing else.

6. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data, this is not applicable.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. All your preferences are stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Clearing Chrome's extension storage data
  • Resetting the extension settings through the popup

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/ordervschaos/pinterest-fullscreen-chrome-plugin/issues
  • GitHub Repository: https://github.com/ordervschaos/pinterest-fullscreen-chrome-plugin
